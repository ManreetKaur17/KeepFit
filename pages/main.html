KeepFit

The fitness app


KeepFit is a fitness app to help people maintain their fitness. It provides different category exercises with their description and video that allows everyone to workout on their own. A person can also post an exercise into the app if they want to help others with their knowledge and skills. It has an inspiration feed to energize people to workout. Users can find parks/gyms nearby their location so that they can choose a place if they don't want to exercise at home. This app also has a BMI calculator that gives an estimate of one's body fat.
